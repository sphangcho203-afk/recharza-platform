# Recharza Brand Identity

Recharza is a clean, modern, multi-game top-up platform. The brand should feel fast, trustworthy, technically capable, and built for serious players without becoming childish, noisy, or overloaded with generic esports effects.

## Core identity

- **Brand name:** Recharza
- **Official styling:** Recharza
- **Uppercase wordmark styling:** RECHARZA
- **Primary tagline:** Play More. Wait Less.
- **Category:** Multi-game top-up and digital gaming commerce
- **Positioning:** A reliable game-credit platform built around clear regional pricing, private order tracking, and controlled fulfilment.

## Brand promise

Recharza should communicate three things immediately:

1. the correct game and regional product;
2. a clear, server-owned price before checkout;
3. a trackable order with visible status and support boundaries.

Do not promise instant delivery, 24/7 support, official publisher partnership, guaranteed refunds, or universal game coverage unless those claims are operationally true and documented.

## Audience

- Mobile Legends and mobile-game players
- Players purchasing game currency, passes, memberships, and gift products
- Customers who care about regional compatibility and transparent order status
- Initially India and Asia-focused, with a global platform identity

## Brand personality

- Clear, calm, and capable
- Gaming-aware but not childish
- Premium without pretending to be luxurious
- Technical without exposing supplier jargon
- Confident without exaggerated claims

### Voice examples

Use:

- Choose the correct market before checkout.
- Your order is saved and ready to track.
- This package is currently under supplier review.
- Payment is not yet enabled for this product.

Avoid:

- Cheapest ever!
- Guaranteed instant delivery!
- Official partner!
- No risk, 100% safe!
- Unlimited everything!

## Visual system

### Primary palette

| Role | Name | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| Main background | Obsidian | `#07070C` |
| Surface | Graphite | `#0E0E15` |
| Primary accent | Electric Violet | `#7C3AED` |
| Secondary accent | Signal Blue | `#5B7CFF` |
| Information accent | Cyan Signal | `#22D3EE` |
| Primary text | Frost | `#F8FAFC` |
| Secondary text | Slate | `#94A3B8` |

Use violet and blue as controlled accents, not as constant neon decoration. Keep the interface mostly dark, spacious, and readable.

### Typography

- Primary: Geist Sans or Inter
- Headings: bold, compact, high-contrast sans serif
- Body: clean sans serif with comfortable line spacing
- Technical labels: monospace only for IDs, order references, status codes, and system data

### Logo direction

The Recharza mark should be:

- a simple geometric `R` monogram;
- recognizable at small profile-picture size;
- independent from any game publisher identity;
- usable as a single-color icon;
- compatible with the RECHARZA wordmark;
- free from mascots, weapons, crowns, shields, random lightning, or fake game imagery.

A subtle charging, signal, or forward-motion cut may be integrated into the `R`, but the mark must remain readable and professional.

### Image rules

- Use real reviewed game artwork and official game logos for catalogue identification.
- Never create fake publisher logos.
- Never imply publisher sponsorship or endorsement.
- Do not use generated mascots as the Recharza identity.
- Social graphics should use one clear subject, one headline, and one action.

## Naming and handle system

Use one username across every platform whenever available.

1. `@recharza`
2. `@recharzastore`
3. `@recharzaofficial`

Avoid numbers, repeated underscores, country suffixes, and platform-specific variations unless unavoidable.

### Email identity

Initial Gmail preference order:

1. `recharza.store@gmail.com`
2. `recharza.support@gmail.com`
3. `recharzaofficial@gmail.com`

Availability must be checked during account creation. Do not use a personal school or gaming email as the public business identity.

Future domain email structure:

- `support@recharza.com`
- `orders@recharza.com`
- `security@recharza.com`
- `partnerships@recharza.com`

## Brand governance

- One profile picture across all public platforms
- One wordmark and one approved color system
- One canonical bio message adapted only for platform length
- Website remains the source of truth for pricing, checkout, order status, terms, and policies
- Social messages must never request passwords, OTPs, card PINs, recovery codes, or account credentials
- Support staff must never ask customers to send payment to an unrelated personal account
